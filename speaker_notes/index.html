Slide 1

Hi. My name is ..., and I am ....

These are your notes, write them the way you want.

Some people write only bullet points, other people write complete sentences, and others write full sentences with bold font on the key elements of each sentence.

That will help avoid perceptible eye movement and reduce the risk of getting lost while speaking.

If you go with full sentences, make sure you use small paragraphs, and don't make blocks bigger than three or four lines. Use html p element to separate paragraphs.

Note that this template sets the text blocks to 500px of width, which may also avoid perceptible eye movement.
